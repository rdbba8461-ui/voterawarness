Simple Steps to Become a Voter

Ready to make your voice heard? Here's how to register for your Voter ID card in India.

  1. 1

    Visit the Official Portal

    Go to the official Voter's Services Portal of the Election Commission of India. This is the only place you need to go to apply.

    voters.eci.gov.in
  2. 2

    Fill out Form 6

    Complete Form 6 for new voter registration. You'll need to provide your personal details and residency information.

  3. 3

    Upload Your Documents

    Be ready to upload digital copies of your passport-size photo and proofs of identity, address, and date of birth.

  4. 4

    Track and Receive

    After submission, you can track the status of your application online. Your Voter ID card will be issued once approved.
